AWS Cloud Club is coming to IGDTUW

Get ready for an exciting and insightful journey with AWS

Through AWS cloud club we wish to explore the world of cloud computing as it is a new and emerging field and AWS is the most used cloud computing services. Students can gain more industrial experience working with AWS and AWS services.

AWS cloud club offers some exciting benefits, such as AWS credits, certification vouchers, exclusive experiences in partnerships with AWS employees, and opportunities for speaking and career related workshops

Students can deploy and integrate their projects using various AWS services and can use to make an impressionable resume.

There will be free credits, vouchers and some amazing goodies for its members along with various speaker sessions getting organized.

About AWS Cloud Club IGDTUW

AWS Cloud Clubs are student-focused, peer-driven user groups for post-secondary-level students worldwide. Run by Cloud Club Captains across the globe. The mission is to help more students expand their career options, increase their technical social circles, and discover ways to engage with AWS as a technical platform.

About AWS

Amazon Web Services(AWS) is a cloud computing platform which aims to provide low cost and on the go services to its customer. It includes a mixture of infrastructure-as-a-service(IaaS), platform-as-a-service(PaaS) and packaged-software-as-a-service(SaaS) offerrings. It has over 200 fully featured services from data centres globally.

  • AWS Cloud Club Orientation - 22 April 2023

    IGDTUW Cloud Club Launch and Orientation

    Date:
    April 22, 2023.
    Platform:
    YouTube live stream
    Guest Speaker:
    Mr. Gaurav Garg
    Find the minutes of meeting here:
    1. The core team of the AWS Club was introduced, comprising members with expertise in cloud computing, management, technical skills, and media.Various benefits were highlighted for students, including AWS certification vouchers, exclusive experiences with AWS, speaking opportunities, career-related workshops, and access to resources and fundingThe club aims to help students in upskilling and preparing for future careers in cloud computing.Cloud computing was emphasized as an in-demand skill that students can develop through the club.Opportunities for networking with industry experts and peers were provided.
    2. Hands-on practical labs were organized for captains and members to learn AWS.The guest speaker shared their experience studying and researching security, particularly in the context of IoT.They discussed a project they worked on during an interview, highlighting the importance of cloud computing in it.
    3. The orientation session provided an introduction to cloud computing, explaining it as the delivery of computing resources over the internet instead of relying on physical hardware.The benefits of cloud computing were discussed, including scalability, cost efficiency, accessibility, flexibility, reliability, security, and ease of management
    4. The event was the launch of the AWS Club of IGDTUW.The club focuses on cloud computing and aims to explore applications and innovation in this field.Cloud computing was described as a group of computers worldwide that store content and provide secure access to it.The speaker highlighted the importance of cloud computing across different fields.Examples were given, such as its relevance in mechanical engineering and machine learning.
    5. Various cloud service providers in the market were mentioned, including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, IBM Cloud, Oracle Cloud, Salesforce Cloud, and Alibaba Cloud.AWS was highlighted as the dominant cloud computing platform due to its early entry, wide range of services, scalability, flexibility, reliability, security, and continuous innovation.AWS was discussed as a platform that can be integrated into various projects, including IoT, machine learning, and web development, to enhance their capabilities and leverage AWS's resources.
    6. A guest speaker, Mr. Gaurav Garg, a technical account manager at AWS, joined the session. The speaker discussed the power and ubiquity of cloud computing, comparing it to electricity as a common utility enabling a wide range of applications. Examples of food aggregators, ride-sharing apps, and pharmaceutical ordering services were given to illustrate the ease and global reach provided by cloud computing. The session ended with an invitation for questions and interaction with the audience.
